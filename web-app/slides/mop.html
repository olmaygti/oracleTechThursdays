MetaProgramming

Introducing MOP

The Meta Object Protocol specifies the method resolution chain in Groovy

  • Devs can easily intercept any method call (AOP)
  • Devs can react to calls to unexistent methods avoiding Exceptions and providing default behaviour (great for DSL's)
  • Devs can even create new methods in RunTime!
  • Take a look at GORM Dynamic Finders
